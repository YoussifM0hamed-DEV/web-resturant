## 2026-05-17 - [Micro-UX: Accessibility and Password Toggle]
**Learning:** Adding ARIA labels to icon-only buttons (like navbar togglers and quantity +/- buttons) significantly improves accessibility for screen reader users. A "Show Password" toggle is a simple but high-impact micro-UX improvement that helps users verify their input and reduces login friction.
**Action:** Always check for icon-only interactive elements and ensure they have descriptive `aria-label` attributes. Implement password visibility toggles on all authentication forms to enhance usability.
